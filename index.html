Summary

I have always been inspired and motivated by the female energy, beauty of their bodies, and power that it posses through life I started to incorporate my love for them Ito my art. Using my favorite way of expression, photography, I take shots that for me speak my vision of power. This project is a product of my inspiration from human connection to nature. Often we adjective it with "mother" nature and I like to think of it as a woman that we all originate from. Mother of the world we live in, a woman that gives life, the power it holds to carry us all. But in our everyday life there are so many women I meet that inspire me with their strength with their life struggles and the ways they keep going.

In this project, I captured my inspirations in their natural habitat, nature, and the way they act in such surroundings I find magical.

About

My name is Ewa Good, a photographer from Poland settled in Copenhagen. Besides a decade of working as a freelance photographer, I express my art through illustrations and tattooing. My compassion gives me the motivation to listen and get inspired by stories and experiences of others, many of my friends found it helpful therefore I started organizing woman sharing circles and give the psychological help through hanging out, speaking out, or just listening.

However, I have many passions and it enables me to get creative in many different spheres and kinds of at. I am always keen to learn and try out something new. If you like my style and have any idea that is open for collaboration do not hesitate to send me an inquiry.
